## 🖥️ Wallpaper Ideas
1. **The MAGI Hex**: Use the Primary Orange and Background Black to create a honeycomb hex pattern.
2. **Unit-01 Glow**: Use the Midnight Purple as a base with a radial Neon Green glow in the center.
3. **The Central Dogma**: Deep dark backgrounds with minimal terminal-style text in the accent colors.
